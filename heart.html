Overview

The heart is a muscular organ responsible for pumping blood throughout the body, delivering oxygen and nutrients. It beats around 100,000 times per day, maintaining systemic and pulmonary circulation.

Key Functions

  • Pumps oxygen-rich blood to tissues
  • Maintains arterial blood pressure
  • Removes metabolic waste via circulation
  • Electrical conduction (SA node, AV node)
