What is the Heart?

The heart is a muscular organ about the size of your fist. It pumps blood through blood vessels to every part of your body. It beats about 100,000 times a day!

Four Chambers

  • Right Atrium – receives deoxygenated blood from body.
  • Right Ventricle – pumps blood to lungs.
  • Left Atrium – receives oxygenated blood from lungs.
  • Left Ventricle – pumps blood to whole body (strongest chamber).

Blood Flow Through Heart

Body → Right Atrium → Right Ventricle → Lungs (oxygen pickup) → Left Atrium → Left Ventricle → Body

Valves prevent backward flow.
