Overview

The brain is the command center of the nervous system. It controls all activities of the body—conscious and unconscious—including thought, memory, emotion, touch, motor skills, vision, breathing, temperature, and every process that regulates our body.

Primary Functions

  • Integrates sensory information
  • Controls voluntary & involuntary movement
  • Stores and retrieves memory
  • Processes cognition, language & emotion
